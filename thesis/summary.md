# Summary

Deep learning has achieved remarkable success in image classification, yet its performance remains heavily dependent on large-scale annotated datasets. Acquiring high-quality labels is expensive and time-consuming, particularly in specialised domains such as medical imaging and remote sensing. Active learning addresses this challenge by intelligently selecting the most informative samples for annotation, thereby maximising model performance under a limited labelling budget. However, conventional active learning methods, which rely on model predictions such as softmax entropy or margin scores to assess sample uncertainty, suffer from a fundamental cold-start dilemma: when very few labels are available, the model itself is poorly trained, rendering its uncertainty estimates unreliable precisely when intelligent sample selection is most needed.

深度学习在图像分类领域取得了显著成功，但其性能仍然高度依赖大规模标注数据集。获取高质量标签既昂贵又耗时，尤其在医学影像和遥感等专业领域更为突出。主动学习通过智能地选择最具信息量的样本进行标注来应对这一挑战，从而在有限的标注预算下最大化模型性能。然而，传统的主动学习方法依赖模型预测（如softmax熵或间隔分数）来评估样本不确定性，面临着根本性的冷启动困境：当可用标签极少时，模型本身训练不充分，其不确定性估计恰恰在最需要智能选样的阶段变得不可靠。

This dissertation presents a feature-space contrastive uncertainty framework for active learning that leverages frozen vision foundation models to circumvent this cold-start problem. Rather than depending on a poorly trained classifier's outputs, the proposed approach operates directly in the high-quality feature space of a frozen DINOv3 Vision Transformer (ViT-B/16), where meaningful distance and neighbourhood relationships exist from the outset. Four complementary uncertainty measures are designed to capture different aspects of sample informativeness: Exploration Uncertainty quantifies a sample's distance from the nearest labelled sample, promoting coverage of unexplored feature-space regions; Boundary Uncertainty measures the label entropy among a sample's K-nearest neighbours, identifying samples near class decision boundaries; Density Uncertainty computes the standard deviation of neighbour similarities to detect isolated or noisy samples; and Multi-Scale Feature Uncertainty evaluates the consistency of uncertainty signals across multiple transformer layers (layers 3, 6, 9, and 11), distinguishing genuinely complex samples from single-layer anomalies.

本文提出了一种基于特征空间的对比不确定性主动学习框架，利用冻结的视觉基础模型来规避冷启动问题。该方法不依赖训练不充分的分类器输出，而是直接在冻结的DINOv3视觉Transformer（ViT-B/16）的高质量特征空间中进行操作，该空间从一开始就具备有意义的距离和邻域关系。本文设计了四种互补的不确定性度量，从不同维度捕捉样本的信息量：探索不确定性量化样本到最近已标注样本的距离，促进对未探索特征空间区域的覆盖；边界不确定性度量样本K近邻中标签的熵值，识别位于类间决策边界附近的样本；密度不确定性计算邻居相似度的标准差，检测孤立或含噪样本；多尺度特征不确定性评估不确定性信号在多个Transformer层（第3、6、9和11层）之间的一致性，将真正复杂的样本与单层异常区分开来。

These four uncertainties are fused through an attention modulation mechanism that dynamically adjusts the weight of each feature uncertainty according to the classifier's prediction entropy. When the classifier is uncertain, exploration and boundary signals are amplified; when it is confident, the weights remain balanced. A density-based noise filter further removes potentially noisy samples before final top-K selection. The complete system operates in a closed-loop fashion: in each round, the frozen DINOv3 extracts features, the Memory Bank stores labelled sample representations, the uncertainty estimator evaluates all unlabelled candidates, and the most valuable samples are selected for annotation and classifier retraining.

这四种不确定性通过注意力调制机制进行融合，该机制根据分类器的预测熵动态调整每种特征不确定性的权重。当分类器不确定时，探索和边界信号的权重被放大；当分类器有信心时，权重保持均衡。基于密度的噪声过滤器在最终的top-K选择之前进一步移除潜在的噪声样本。整个系统以闭环方式运行：在每一轮中，冻结的DINOv3提取特征，记忆库存储已标注样本的表示，不确定性估计器评估所有未标注候选样本，选出最有价值的样本进行标注和分类器重训练。

Experiments are conducted on ImageNet-100 (100 classes, approximately 101,000 training images). Using only 5% of the training data (5,060 images selected over 10 rounds), the proposed method achieves a validation accuracy of 85.29%, outperforming random sampling by 4.10 percentage points (81.19%). Systematic ablation studies demonstrate that each uncertainty component contributes positively: removing Exploration Uncertainty causes the largest drop (−1.23%), followed by Multi-Scale (−0.95%), Boundary (−0.89%), and Density (−0.31%). The framework is also validated with a ResNet50 backbone, where it achieves 53.89% versus 50.66% for random sampling (+3.23%), confirming its generalisability across feature extractors of varying capacity.

实验在ImageNet-100数据集（100个类别，约101,000张训练图像）上进行。仅使用5%的训练数据（经10轮选择共5,060张图像），所提方法达到85.29%的验证准确率，超过随机采样4.10个百分点（81.19%）。系统性的消融实验表明每种不确定性组件均有正向贡献：移除探索不确定性造成最大性能下降（−1.23%），其次是多尺度（−0.95%）、边界（−0.89%）和密度（−0.31%）。该框架在ResNet50骨干网络上也得到验证，取得53.89%的准确率，而随机采样为50.66%（+3.23%），证实了框架在不同能力特征提取器上的通用性。
